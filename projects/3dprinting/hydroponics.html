Hydroponic System

Status: Testing

Concept

From my desire to grow hot peppers and the constraints imposed by a small space, my hydroponic system was born. A soilless, contained method of growing plants that could be easily serviceable, portable, configurable, and extensible seemed like the most ideal way to begin my horticultural journey.

Creation

Since a compact setup was the highest priority requirement, I began with a jar and a small, submersible water pump.
