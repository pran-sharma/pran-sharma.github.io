Digital Sculpting

Jewelry Design

Pendant

The pendant was designed in virtual reality using Gravity Sketch then sent to a casting company, where the model was 3D printed in wax, then cast in bronze.

Once the casting was received, the metal needed to be sanded and polished, and then a stone was set in the prongs.

Engagement Ring

The ring was designed in virtual reality using Gravity Sketch then sent to a casting company, where the model was 3D printed in wax, then cast in platinum.

Once the casting was received, the metal needed to be sanded and polished, and then a stone was set in the prongs.

Vehicle Rebadge

The emblem was sculpted in Blender and realized on a consumer grade FDM printer.
