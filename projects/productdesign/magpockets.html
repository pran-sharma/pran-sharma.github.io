MagPockets/MagnaPockets/Phockets

Status: Product Testing

Concept

A removable pocket would be a fun new way to accessorize, especially for men, who often shy away from more flashy accessories like jewelry or bags, as well as a practical storage space for women, whose pants pockets are generally anemic if not nonexistent.

Creation

I began by ideating how I might create a comfortable, robust, nondestructive method of fastening a pocket to another fabric.
