Custom Electric Bicycle

Origins

After moving to Seattle and losing my car on the way (see video), I obviously found it more difficult to get around. I had to rely on public transportation and my own two feet, and felt myself longing for an easier, more independent mode of travel around the city and beyond, potentially even to commute to and from work. Due to the high cost to my bank account and the environment, replacing my car was out of the question. I instead considered personal electric vehicles such as motorized scooters, skateboards, and bicycles, and figured a bike would best fit my critera, weighting range heaviest in my decision. Looking into different ebikes, I discovered that options within my budget, while meeting my range requirements, did not fit my aesthetic appeal whatsoever. As an avid DIYer, I thought it could be a fun, fulfilling project to build my own ebike, and I could make it look however I'd like and exactly meet my power and range requirements, while also being easily upgraded if necessary.
