SmartLock/OpenLock/ModLock

Status: Work in Progress

Concept

A bolt-on smart lock that could be as *smart or dumb* as you need would be a great *quality of life* improvement for myself and most people.

At every apartment I've ever lived in, I wished my door's lock could do more- from unlocking/locking remotely/automatically to monitoring the outside world. Sure, video doorbells and smart locks are on the market, but they're not very renter-friendly, they all require different apps, they're not entirely configurable, and being closed source they could compromise your security.

Creation

As my first endeavor into the world of the internet of things, I began with a simple exercise to allow my microcontroller to connect to my network and host a web portal I may use to control, configure, and monitor my lock. The microcontroller first broadcasts a network which hosts a web portal where a user may scan for and join their own network. Once connected, it then hosts the lock's command center.
